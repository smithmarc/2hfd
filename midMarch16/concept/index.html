Concept:

2Hfd is a youth football development school, we are committed to creating and developing young footballers between the ages of 6 - 17.

Our aim is to nurture young players from an early age and give them good grounding to follow the dream of becoming a professional player.

We recognise the importance of developing a player during the key years and understand how technique along with skill are the main attributes to a player under 17 years of age.

We understand players will develop at their own pace and it is important that we encourage players to accept mistakes, showing winning is not important.

It is about the fundamental skills to reach their full potential, each individual player will follow the football pathway and be developed for each stage.
